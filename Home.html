KidControl

On busy days, parents are often focused on their work responsibilities, striving to earn a living, which leads to the situation that they have limited time for their children. During school days parents face challenges managing dropping off and picking up their children on time. In many cases, it's during these unsupervised moments that the risk of kidnapping increases, as perpetrators take advantage of the lack of parental presence. To maintain children's safety during these important times of day, more monitoring is required, as well as possible technology solutions.
